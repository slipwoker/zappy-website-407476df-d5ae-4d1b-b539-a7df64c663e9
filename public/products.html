    Care Instructions

    Bamboo Heaven sheets are designed for easy care and long-lasting beauty. Follow these simple guidelines to keep your sheets looking and feeling their best:

    Washing

    Machine wash cold or warm with like colors. Use mild, eco-friendly detergent without fabric softeners.

    Drying

    Tumble dry on low heat or line dry for best results. Remove promptly to minimize wrinkles.

    Avoid

    Do not bleach, use fabric softener, or dry clean. These can damage the bamboo fibers and reduce softness.

    Pro Tip

    Your sheets will become softer with each wash. For best results, wash before first use.
